About PHOENIX
My name is Phoenix(Wang Zhi) and I am a freelance photographer with tremendous passion and sincerity in photography. I believe happiness can be delivered through the pictures I captured. For the momentous meaning of wedding to every person, I am so fascinated by the role as a wedding photographer. I dedicate myself to every wedding and use my photos to keep the unique moments for every couple. My goal is to provide you with professionalism, creativity and quality. I believe that wedding photography is a vision that not only created by photographer but also the couple. Therefore I seek to establish a strong personal connection with all of my clients and give the best images with my distinguished photography style as well as client expectation.
Besides the wedding related shooting experiences, I also handled with photography in baby portrait, commercial advertising, event documentary and so many diverse areas. A traveler who is addicted to capturing landscape and people can also pretty well define me. I am connected to many magazines in China and lots of my works were posted in the past few years. At the meantime, I am also a Gettyimages contributor who sells the pictures in the biggest network library in the world.
